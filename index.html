Risk-Reward Indicator

The Risk-Reward Indicator is an essential tool for Forex traders, designed to measure the potential profit against the potential loss in any given trade. It helps traders assess the balance between the risk they are taking and the possible reward they can achieve, which is crucial for making informed and disciplined trading decisions.

HTF-Speed

High-Frequency Trading (HFT) leverages sophisticated algorithms and powerful computers to execute a large number of trades in fractions of a second, significantly enhancing trading efficiency and market liquidity.

Candle Time End and Spread Indicator

The Candle Time End and Spread Indicator displays the remaining time until the current candlestick closes and the current spread value, helping traders make timely and informed decisions based on these key pieces of information.

Object Manager Indicator (Color levels)

The Object Manager Indicator with Color Levels allows traders to draw and customize various shapes and lines on price charts, such as trend lines, support/resistance levels, and rectangles, using different colors to highlight key price action patterns and improve technical analysis.
